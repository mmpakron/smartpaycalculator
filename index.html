How it works

The simple math behind the calculator.

Hourly → Salary

Annual = Hourly × Hours per Week × Weeks per Year Monthly = Annual ÷ 12 Weekly = Annual ÷ Weeks per Year

Salary → Hourly

Hourly = Annual Salary ÷ (Hours per Week × Weeks per Year)

This calculator does not include taxes or benefits. It’s meant for quick pay comparisons.

FAQ

Common questions people ask.

Does this include taxes or deductions?

No. This shows gross pay only (before taxes, deductions, retirement, insurance, etc.).

What if I don’t work 52 weeks per year?

Change “Weeks per year” to match your situation. For example, 50 weeks accounts for ~2 weeks unpaid time off.

What about overtime, bonuses, or commission?

This tool assumes consistent hours and pay. For overtime or variable income, we can add a separate calculator later.

Why does the monthly number look slightly “off” sometimes?

Monthly is calculated as annual ÷ 12. Some employers pay biweekly or semi-monthly, which can differ slightly.
